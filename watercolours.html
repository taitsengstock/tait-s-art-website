description

Someone sits on anotherâ€™s shoulders. Surrounded by a crowd. Is this the scene of a music concert? The elevated figure wears a jersey similar to a classic football jumper made of wool with an itchy collar. Is she being cheered for her 100th game? Or is this a violent scene? a riot? Untitled #2

watercolour on paper
630mm x 785mm
2016

Description

A woman bare breasted sits in a car a hand out the window. She might have a cigarette between her fingers, I detect a sexual air. She is comfortable and gazes out into the starry deep blue sky. It is the end of an encounter between her and the shadowed figure lurking behind her. Our puzzle is what went on? What does her expression and body language tell us about the previous encounter?

Untitled #4

watercolour on paper
705mm x 885mm
2016

Description

This could be the image of two women or maybe just one viewed twice in the same picture plane. From behind she seems well groomed, tall posture wearing a fashionable top that sits on one shoulder. The next figure seems dirty, strained and intense wearing a loose t-shirt. Something mysterious is happening between them. There is a transparency and a transition between the two that is hard to grasp.
