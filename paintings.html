Portrait of a man

Oil on canvas
200cm X 290cm
2016

Description

A man floats alone in a richly coloured turquoise space. Anxious and illuminated, he gazes at something below. In making this picture I was simply trying to convey a scene at a swimming pool. The artificial chlorinated water and the bathing head. But something more ominous emerged; more representational of anxiety, alienation and perhaps the feeling of claustrophobia.

Untitled

Oil on canvas
187cm x 267cm
2016

Description

A woman faces towards a distant horizon, a city most likely, pulsating with light and energy, almost fluid. Between her and the city is a body of water and a rock floating in mid air. The woman, a traveller, her belongings all fit within her shoulder bag, is eager to arrive at her destination. The rock has a symbolic quality, conveying a message and a clue, a final obstacle.

Untitled

Oil on canvas
2013cm X 4240cm
2017

Description

We probably pass this image a lot in everyday life. A person deciding between products in a supermarket, or waiting at traffic lights or for a bus, turning thoughts over inside their head. She wears a hoodie over her brightly coloured gym clothes, something more modest for a public space. The sweat from her workout is cooling her in the breeze. Although there is a space beyond the picture frame and she faces outwards, she seems to be looking at something within her herself. And where are we?
